McChicken

A McDonald's classic!

Mcdonald's classic chicken sandwich with shredded lettuce and mayo.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Iceberg Lettuce(to taste)
  • Canola oil (as needed)
  • Hamburger buns (to taste)

Chicken Patty

  • Boneless chicken breasts(3)
  • egg(1)
  • salt(1/2tsp)
  • corn starch (1 cup)

Chicken Batter

  • water (1 cup)
  • all-purpose flour (1cup)
  • corn starch (1/2 cup)
  • egg (1)
  • yellow mustard (2 tbsp)
  • garlic powder (1 tbsp)
  • onion powder (1 tbsp)
  • salt (1 tsp)
  • ground black pepper (1 tsp)

Special Sauce

  • mayonnaise (1cup)
  • dry mustard (1 tsp)
  • onion powder (1/2 tbsp)
  • salt (1/2 tsp)

Instructions

  1. Add three boneless, skinless chicken breasts, one egg, and half a teaspoon of salt to a food processor. Pulse the mixture until it's smooth.
  2. Place one cup of corn starch in a bowl. Shape the chicken patties with moist palms so that they are slightly thicker than the bun and slightly bigger than it. After coating both sides, put them aside.
  3. Add the following ingredients to a large mixing bowl: 1 cup water, 1 cup all-purpose flour, 1/2 cup corn starch, 1 egg, 2 tablespoons yellow mustard, 1 tablespoon garlic powder, 1 tablespoon onion powder, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1 teaspoon ground black pepper. Until the batter is smooth, whisk it.
  4. Apply the batter to the chicken patties. After removing the excess, transfer to a baking tray covered with parchment paper. To harden, place in the freezer for one to two hours.
  5. To prepare the sauce, combine the mayonnaise (1 cup), salt (1/2 tsp), onion powder (1/2 Tbsp), and dry mustard (1 tsp) in a small mixing dish. Stir thoroughly and reserve.
  6. When necessary, add canola oil to a deep fryer or large saucepan and heat it to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius).
  7. For five minutes, fry the chicken patties.
  8. Patties should rest for three minutes on a platter covered with paper towels.
  9. Cook patties for a further five minutes, or until exteriors are golden brown. After draining, use paper towels to pat dry.
  10. Toast hamburger buns to taste
  11. Put the chicken patty on the heel bun. Spread the special sauce over the crown and sprinkle the shredded iceberg lettuce on top (to taste).
