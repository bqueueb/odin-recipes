McChicken

A McDonald's classic!

Mcdonald's classic chicken sandwich with shredded lettuce and mayo.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Iceberg Lettuce(to taste)
  • Canola oil (as needed)
  • Hamburger buns (to taste)

Chicken Patty

  • Boneless chicken breasts(3)
  • egg(1)
  • salt(1/2tsp)
  • corn starch (1 cup)

Chicken Batter

  • water (1 cup)
  • all-purpose flour (1cup)
  • corn starch (1/2 cup)
  • egg (1)
  • yellow mustard (2 tbsp)
  • garlic powder (1 tbsp)
  • onion powder (1 tbsp)
  • salt (1 tsp)
  • ground black pepper (1 tsp)

Special Sauce

  • mayonnaise (1cup)
  • dry mustard (1 tsp)
  • onion powder (1/2 tbsp)
  • salt (1/2 tsp)
